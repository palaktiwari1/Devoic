Privacy Policy

At Devoic, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy as you embark on your journey to becoming a NextGen Solopreneur. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

  • Information We Collect:


    We collect information to enhance your experience and provide tailored services. This may include:

    • Personal Information: Name, contact details, and other identifying information for enrollment and communication purposes.
    • Course Progress Data: Information related to your progress in skill-building courses and bootcamps.
    • Portfolio Projects: Details of projects created during your learning journey.
    • Interaction Data: Information generated through your participation in our community, mentorship, and networking activities.

  • How We Use Your Information:


    • Personalization: Tailoring our programs and services to meet your specific needs and preferences.
    • Communication: Keeping you informed about course updates, mentorship opportunities, and community events.
    • Improvement: Analyzing data to enhance our offerings, curriculum, and overall user experience.

  • Data Security:


    We prioritize the security of your information. We implement industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction of data.

  • Third-Party Services:


    Devoic may use third-party services to deliver certain aspects of our programs, such as payment processing or analytics. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

  • Your Choices:


    You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Additionally, you can manage your communication preferences through your account settings.

  • Sharing Your Success Stories:


    With your consent, we may share your success stories and portfolio projects on our website or promotional materials to inspire and motivate others.

    • By engaging with Devoic, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy periodically, and any changes will be reflected on our website. For any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please contact us at contact@devoic.com.
