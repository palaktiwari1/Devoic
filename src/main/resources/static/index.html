About

Find Out More About Us

Devoic provides the programs, services, and support to guide aspiring solopreneurs on their journey from skills development to successful launch.

Our Mission

We believe solopreneurship helps individuals unlock their full potential. Our mission is to provide aspiring solopreneurs with everything they need to successfully start their own ventures or leverage those skills in a full-time role.

  • What We Offer

    • - Skill-building courses & bootcamps in areas like digital marketing, design, web development, UI/UX Design, No-Code Dev, Prompt Engineering
    • - 1:1 mentorship from successful solopreneurs
    • - Building real-world projects for your portfolio
    • - IT services like web and app development, analytics
    • - Content creation services including graphic design, copywriting, video production
    • - Tight-knit community, networking, peer support
    • - Job/freelancing placement support

  • The Devoic Journey- from enrollment to launch, we guide you through every step:

    • - Take courses to gain core solopreneur skills
    • - Get matched with 1:1 expert mentorship
    • - Build portfolio projects and credentials
    • - Leverage our IT and content creation services
    • - Join our online community for feedback and networking
    • - Get assistance with job applications or independent business launch
